Our Story

Established in 2006 in Chicago, our company has specialized in the areas of lighting installation, electric shades, and home integration. We are committed to developing innovative solutions that enhance these fields and improve the overall experience for our customers. Our dedication to creating beautiful and functional spaces has enabled us to build a strong reputation for quality and efficiency in our work.

In 2013, a relocation to the Greater Toronto Area marked the beginning of our commitment to delivering exceptional services to our clients. We emphasize the importance of respecting and safeguarding our customers’ homes throughout each project. With a focus on precision, we ensure that results meet the highest standards. Furthermore, we offer continuous support and remain adaptable to accommodate the evolving needs of our clients.
