Lighting Installation

Lighting is one of the most important, yet so subtle, elements in the overall interior design of any space. Cove lighting, monorail, recessed fixtures, green technology LEDs; fluorescent accent; decorative wall fixtures; each plays its role in bringing the best in each piece of art, furniture or just in the overall aspect of the room.

Lighting Control

The Lighting Control Systems allows you to control and program the lighting from one room or the entire house from wall-mounted keypads. They have a sharp, clean look and are illuminated with custom-engraved preset scenes. Save money, energy, improve aesthetics and be safe in your home when you try the new way to control light fixtures.

Motorized Fans

Enjoy year-round comfort with ceiling fan installation, and take control of your home with smart automation systems that integrate lights, climate, and security.
